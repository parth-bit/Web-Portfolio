Welcome to My Journey

Hello Visitors, Scroll down to know about my life story, the journey from newbie to an EMERGING Full-stack Developer.

CHAPTER 1- BIRTH 2003

Born on September 2003 in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh into a joint family of 26 members. I was the naughtest & laziest one in the family. It was a pleasure to born in such a joyful house. I am the most loved kid. I have a young sister too, she is the most irritating creature I ever got.

Chapter 2- New City

Life is all about ups and downs, Me and my family shifted to Raipur, Chattisgarh. New state, New City, New school made me nervous. But I never gave up. I was in 3rd grade where some subjects are new to me like HINDI. I faced difficulty to understand Hindi but somehow I managed. I became school topper and proved myself that I can face challenges and realised that
" I AM A GOOD LEARNER."

Chapter 3- Interests

I was a student in Adarsh Vidyalaya School and I still remember I was in 9th grade when we were taught about HTML. I was amazed by developing small websites and my teacher even taught how to animate websites. I was so amazed that my interest in web designing and developing started increasing. I begin to learn more and more, developed some websites as well as learned many languages like Python, Java and C. Developed many projects. I somewhere heard about Full-stack Deveopment and then my life took a turn. !!

Chapter 4- College Life

After completing my school, I decided that
"I want to become a FULL-STACK DEV"
I moved to Bangalore for my Under graduation and took admission in Presidency College. I successfully completed Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications with a outstanding CGPA of 9.0 in 2024 . Surviving in Bangalore is tough, people eat rice thrice a day. I missed home specially when it's a festival because this city has nothing to do in occasions. Still managed to survive and learned a lot from this city.
